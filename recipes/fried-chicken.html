Fried Chicken

Description

golden and crispy crust with juicy meat underneath

you won't be able to resist!

Ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • cornstarch
  • soy sauce
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. soak the chicken in soy sauce for 1-2 hours
  2. make a 50:50 mix of flour and cornstarch to cover chicken with
  3. add salt to the mixture
  4. rub the chicken in the flour-starch mixture untill fully covered
  5. heat up the oil then fry in batches

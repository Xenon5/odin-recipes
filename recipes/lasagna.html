Lasagna

Description

very delicious and full of cheese

the simplest lasagna ever

Ingredients

  • parmesan cheese
  • salt
  • butter
  • ground beef
  • lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. layer the lasagna sheets in baking pan
  2. spread the ground beef, butter, and cheese
  3. add a few tbsp salt
  4. continue layering until stacked to desired height
  5. put in oven and bake at 400F for 25mins

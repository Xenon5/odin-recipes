Miso Soup

Description

flavorful and healthy miso soup

good paired with any meal

Ingredients

  • water
  • miso paste
  • seaweed
  • tofu
  • chopped scallions

Steps

  1. add the water and tofu to pot and bring to a boil
  2. then turn down the heat and add the seaweed
  3. once seaweed has been expanded, turn off heat
  4. mix in the miso paste
  5. sprinkle scallions in soup and serve
